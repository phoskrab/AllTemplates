National Museum Phnom Penh Tour

You will have a better understandable about khmer culture and history of art of Cambodia. The National Museum of Cambodia is located just north of Royal Palace. It was established by king Sisowat in 1920. It is contents the world's largest of Khmer art. There are more than 5,000 artworks from 7th to 13th century. It is included sculpture, ceramics, bronzes, and ethnographic objects dating from prehistoric times to periods before, during, and after the Khmer Empire. It is open daily from 8:00am to 5:00pm.
